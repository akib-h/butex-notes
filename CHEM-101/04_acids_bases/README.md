## 🔑 Key Formulas at a Glance

### Brønsted-Lowry Acid-Base Reaction
$$\ce{HA + B <=> A^- + HB^+}$$

### Acid Dissociation Constant
$$K_a = \frac{[\ce{H^+}][\ce{A^-}]}{[\ce{HA}]} \qquad \mathrm{p}K_a = -\log K_a$$

### Water Ion Product
$$K_w = [\ce{H^+}][\ce{OH^-}] = 1.0 \times 10^{-14} \quad (25^\circ\mathrm{C})$$

### Henderson-Hasselbalch Equation (Buffer)
$$\mathrm{pH} = \mathrm{p}K_a + \log\frac{[\ce{A^-}]}{[\ce{HA}]}$$

### Lewis Acid-Base Reaction
$$\text{Acid (e}^- \text{ acceptor)} + \text{Base (e}^- \text{ donor)} \rightarrow \text{Adduct}$$

### HSAB Principle
**Hard acids prefer hard bases**  
**Soft acids prefer soft bases**
