## Key Concepts Covered

- **Law of Mass Action** — historical context, rate expressions, derivation of $K$
- **Equilibrium Constant** — $K_c$, $K_p$, $K^\circ$, reaction quotient $Q$, $\Delta G^\circ = -RT\ln K$
- **Applications** — PCl₅ dissociation, Haber process, HI formation, N₂O₄ dissociation, Contact process
- **Heterogeneous Equilibrium** — active mass rules, $K_{sp}$, CaCO₃ decomposition, water-gas reaction
- **Le Chatelier's Principle** — concentration, pressure, temperature effects; Haber, Contact, Ostwald processes
