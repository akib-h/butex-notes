## Topics Covered

### 1. Introduction to Chemical Bonding
- Fundamental concepts
- Energy considerations in bonding
- Types of chemical bonds
- Historical development of bonding theories

### 2. Ionic Bonding
- Formation of ionic bonds
- Lattice energy calculations
- Born-Haber cycle
- Properties of ionic compounds

### 3. Covalent Bonding
- Formation and characteristics
- Lewis structures
- Bond parameters (length, energy, polarity)
- Polar and nonpolar bonds

### 4. Metallic Bonding
- Electron sea model
- Band theory basics
- Properties of metals

### 5. VSEPR Theory
- Principles and postulates
- Molecular geometries
- Effect of lone pairs
- Bond angles

### 6. Valence Bond Theory
- Orbital overlap concept
- Hybridization (sp, sp², sp³, sp³d, sp³d²)
- Sigma and pi bonds
- Applications

### 7. Molecular Orbital Theory
- MO formation from atomic orbitals
- Bonding and antibonding orbitals
- Bond order calculations
- Magnetic properties
- Comparison with VBT

### 8. Intermolecular Forces
- Van der Waals forces
- Dipole-dipole interactions
- Hydrogen bonding
- London dispersion forces

### 9. Advanced Topics
- Resonance structures
- Molecular polarity
- Hypervalency
- Exceptions to octet rule
