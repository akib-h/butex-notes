## APPENDIX: CITATION FORMAT GUIDANCE
## ─────────────────────────────────────────

### How to Cite on Slides

For presentation slides, use **shortened in-text format** at the bottom of each slide:

> *(McKinney et al., Nature, 2020)* — for academic papers
> *(Goldman Sachs Research, 2023)* — for industry reports
> *(BBC / Reuters / NYT, date)* — for news sources

### How to Cite in Spoken Script

When speaking, cite conversationally:
- **"A study published in *Nature* in 2020 found that..."**
- **"According to a 2023 Goldman Sachs report..."**
- **"MIT researchers demonstrated in 2018 that..."**
- Do NOT read out full URLs or page numbers verbally
