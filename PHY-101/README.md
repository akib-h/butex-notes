## 🧮 Key Formulas Quick Reference

### Elasticity

$$
\sigma = \frac{F}{A}, \quad \varepsilon = \frac{\Delta L}{L}, \quad E = \frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon}
$$

### Fluid Mechanics

$$
\eta = \frac{\tau}{\dot{\gamma}} = \frac{F/A}{dv/dx}
\qquad \text{(Dynamic Viscosity)}
$$

$$
A_1 v_1 = A_2 v_2
\qquad \text{(Continuity Equation)}
$$

$$
P + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2 + \rho gh = \text{constant}
\qquad \text{(Bernoulli's Equation)}
$$

$$
F = 6\pi\eta r v
\qquad \text{(Stokes' Law)}
$$

### Interference of Light

$$
\beta = \frac{\lambda D}{d}
\qquad \text{(Fringe Width — Young's Experiment)}
$$

$$
D_n^2 = 4n\lambda R
\qquad \text{(Newton's Rings — Dark Rings)}
$$

## 🧵 Textile Relevance

Physics I is foundational for textile engineering. Key connections:

| Physics Concept | Textile Application |
|:----------------|:--------------------|
| **Elasticity / Young's Modulus** | Fiber tensile strength, yarn elongation, fabric stretch & recovery |
| **Viscosity** | Dye liquor flow in wet processing, sizing paste consistency |
| **Surface Tension** | Wetting agents in dyeing, capillary wicking in fibers |
| **Fluid Flow (Bernoulli)** | Air-jet loom nozzle design, pneumatic fiber transport |
| **Reynolds Number** | Flow regime in dyeing machines, jigger vs jet dyeing |
| **Interference of Light** | Fabric color perception, iridescence in synthetic fibers, optical testing |

## 💡 Study Tips

1. **Elasticity** — Memorize the three moduli (Young's, Bulk, Shear) and their units. Practice stress-strain graph interpretation.
2. **Fluid Mechanics** — Derive Bernoulli's theorem at least once from energy principles. Understand when to apply Stokes' law vs Poiseuille's law.
3. **Optics** — Draw ray diagrams for every setup (Young's, Fresnel, Newton's). Most numericals are straightforward once the geometry is clear.
4. **Cross-link** — After finishing viscosity, re-read the textile finishing examples to solidify application context.
5. **Past Papers** — Solve at least 3 years of university questions before exams. Patterns repeat significantly.
