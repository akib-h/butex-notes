# Hemp Fibre  
**Date:** April 5, 2026  

## 1. Botanical Source
- Derived from the stalk of *Cannabis sativa L.* plant.

## 2. Extraction & Processing
1. **Retting** – microbial breakdown of pectin.  
2. **Scutching** – removal of woody core.  
3. **Hackling** – combing to align fibres.  

## 3. Properties
1. Strong and durable.  
2. Resistant to UV light and saltwater.  
3. Eco-friendly: requires less water and pesticides than cotton.  

![Hemp fibre strands](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4a/Hemp_fibre.jpg)

## 4. Applications
- Ropes, textiles, composites, eco-friendly fabrics.  
- Hemp denim as a sustainable alternative to cotton.  

## 5. Example
- **Levi’s Hemp Jeans** – blending hemp with cotton for sustainability.
