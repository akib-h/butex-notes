# Banana Fibre  
**Date:** April 5, 2026  

## 1. Source
- Extracted from pseudostem of banana plant.

## 2. Extraction Methods
- Mechanical scraping, retting, chemical treatment.  

## 3. Properties
1. Strong and biodegradable.  
2. Glossy appearance, similar to silk.  
3. Resistant to saltwater.  

![Banana fibre extraction](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/9f/Banana_fibre.jpg)

## 4. Applications
- Ropes, mats, blended textiles.  
- Banana silk sarees in India.  

## 5. Example
- **Banana fibre sarees** from Tamil Nadu.
