# Kapok Fibre  
**Date:** April 5, 2026  

## 1. Source
- Derived from fruit of *Ceiba pentandra* tree.

## 2. Properties
1. Hollow, lightweight.  
2. Hydrophobic and buoyant.  
3. Antibacterial properties.  

![Kapok fibre](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8f/Kapok_fibre.jpg)

## 3. Applications
- Mattresses, pillows, insulation.  
- Life jackets due to buoyancy.  

## 4. Example
- **Kapok-filled cushions** in eco-friendly furniture.
