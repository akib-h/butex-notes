# Sisal Fibre  
**Date:** April 5, 2026  

## 1. Source
- Extracted from leaves of *Agave sisalana*.

## 2. Properties
1. Coarse and durable.  
2. High tensile strength.  
3. Resistant to saltwater.  

![Sisal fibre](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2f/Sisal_fibre.jpg)

## 3. Applications
- Ropes, mats, composites.  
- Carpets in eco-friendly interiors.  

## 4. Example
- **Sisal rugs** used in sustainable home décor.
