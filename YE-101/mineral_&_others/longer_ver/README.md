## 🔑 Exam Fast Facts

1. **Only mineral fibre** that forms flexible, weavable fibres → **Asbestos**
2. **Strongest & stiffest** natural fibre → **Ramie**
3. **Lightest** natural fibre (lowest density ~0.4 g/cm³) → **Kapok**
4. **Cannot be spun alone** (no inter-fibre cohesion) → **Kapok**
5. **Highest lignin content** (~40–45%) → **Coir**
6. **Lowest lignin content** (~0.6–1%) → **Ramie**
7. **Highest elongation** among natural fibres → **Coir** (15–40%)
8. **Most dangerous asbestos** type → **Crocidolite** (blue asbestos)
9. **Most commercially used asbestos** → **Chrysotile** (white, ~95% of production)
10. **Disease caused by asbestos inhalation** → **Asbestosis** (pulmonary fibrosis)
11. **Piña fabric** is made from → **Pineapple (PALF)**
12. **Hemp vs Marijuana** → Hemp has < 0.3% THC; marijuana has 3–15% THC
13. **Coir is extracted from** → coconut fruit husk (*Cocos nucifera*)
14. **Ramie is also called** → China grass, rhea, grass cloth
15. **Largest producer of coir** → India (~65% of world production)
