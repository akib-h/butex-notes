## ⚙️ Equipment Covered

### 🔥 Boilers (`01_boilers.md`)
Study of steam generators used in textile plants. Covers fire-tube boiler principles, furnace combustion, and the draught chimney height formula. Fire-tube boilers are the industry standard in small to medium-scale textile mills for dyeing, drying, and finishing processes.

### 💧 Pumps (`02_pumps.md`)
Comparison of centrifugal and reciprocating pump mechanisms. Centrifugal pumps use Bernoulli's principle via an impeller; reciprocating pumps use a crank-piston mechanism. Includes discharge calculation and slip percentage formulas.

### 🔩 Engines (`03_engines.md`)
Overview of Internal Combustion (IC) and External Combustion (EC) engines. Detailed comparison of Spark Ignition (Otto/Petrol) and Compression Ignition (Diesel/CI) engines, with Otto Cycle thermal efficiency derivation.

### ❄️ Refrigeration (`04_refrigeration.md`)
Four-stage Vapour Compression Refrigeration Cycle: compression → condensation → throttling → evaporation. Covers the Coefficient of Performance (COP) formula for the ideal Carnot cycle.

### 🌀 Turbines (`05_turbines.md`)
Side-by-side study of Steam Turbines (Rankine Cycle) and Gas Turbines (Brayton Cycle). Covers working fluid, startup characteristics, size, and industrial applications for each type.

## 📐 Formulae Quick Reference

| Topic | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| Draught Height | $h = 353H \left( \frac{1}{T_1} - \frac{m+1}{mT_2} \right)$ |
| Pump Discharge (Single-acting) | $Q_{th} = \frac{LAN}{60}$ |
| Pump Slip | $\text{Slip} = \frac{Q_{th} - Q_a}{Q_{th}} \times 100\%$ |
| Otto Cycle Efficiency | $\eta = 1 - \frac{1}{r^{\gamma - 1}}$ |
| COP (Refrigeration) | $\text{COP} = \frac{T_L}{T_H - T_L}$ |
